Additional Information

Google Glass is a type of wearable technology that offers all the benefits of a smart phone in a hands free style. Google Glass interacts with the user through voice control. Google has sold beta Glass units to qualified "Glass Explorers" who are basically testing out the product.

Google offers four prescription frame options for Glass at a cost of $225 or free with the purchase of Glass. Google has partnered with the owners of Rayban and Oakley to offer top quality frames for they're glasses.

Google Glass was designed by a Google sub-group that focuses on technological advancements such as driverless cars. Google glass is essentially regular glasses that have exchanged the lenses for a Heads up Display. Google Glass is lighter than the average sunglasses.

  • 640 X 360 Himax HX7309 LCoS display
  • 5-megapixel camera, capable of 720p video recording
  • Wi-Fi 802.11b/g
  • Bluetooth
  • 16GB storage (12 GB available including OS)
  • Texas Instruments OMAP 4430 SoC 1.2Ghz Dual(ARMv7)
  • 2GB RAM
  • 3 axis gyroscope
  • 3 axis accelerometer
  • 3 axis magnetometer
  • Ambient light sensing and proximity sensor
  • Bone conduction audio transducer
