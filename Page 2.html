Article Summary

Google CEO Larry Page is selling Google Glass to anyone who can think of a unique use for the glasses and who can also afford the $1500 cost.

It is unsure of what role marketers will play on Google Glass. Google mentioned that they do not intend on displaying digital ads on Google Glass. Also it was mentioned at a conference that Google held that Twitter, Facebook, Tumblr, Evernote, CNN and Elle are building apps.

Google's long term plans for Glass and it's advertising strategy is unclear.
